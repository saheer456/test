About Carevia Foundation

Carevia Foundation began during the COVID-19 crisis with a small group helping struggling families. Over time, it grew into a registered nonprofit working to empower women, support education, and improve healthcare in underserved communities.

The story of Carevia Foundation began in the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic. A small group of friends came together with one simple intention ‚Äî to stand beside those who were struggling in the most difficult of times. What started as small acts of kindness and support during a global crisis soon grew into a shared commitment to create lasting change. Over the course of six years, that spirit of compassion evolved into something greater: Carevia Foundation. Today, we are a registered nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering women, nurturing education, and expanding access to healthcare in underserved communities.
